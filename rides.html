Registration

Starts from: 30 August, 2018 at 8pm AEST

Total Cost: $950

1st Installment: $300, August 2018

2st Installment: $650, November 2018

Note: 1st instalment of $300 is NON REFUNDABLE . Due to some compulsory accommodation fees already paid for by Audax Tasmania.

Pre-qualifying Ride (1)

  • A 600Km (or greater) Audax Australia ride (or international ACP/BRM/LRM) 1st May 2018 to 31st January 2019.
  • Or an Audax Australia 600km Permanent 1st May 2018 to 31st January 2019, must check with Ride Organiser first.

Event Tracking

We would like riders to participate in tracking, note that this is not compulsory:

SPOT Tracker or mobile phone app

> Bubbler GPS (Android)

> SpotWalla (Apple iOS).

Audax Australia Safety Recommendations

1. Front and rear lights that complies with Australian Road Rules Safety Recommendations.

2. Reflective vest or equivalent suggested by Audax Australia Safety Recommendations.

Note: New Audax Australia Ride Rules from 1 November 2018

Suggested Additional Carriage of Clothing & Equipment

  • Space Blanket (300, 400 and 600km rides).
  • Suitable long sleeved rain jacket.
  • Woollen under vest or equivalent.
  • Leg & arm warmers (or equivalent), booties, beanie (or equivalent), & long finger gloves.

Additional Audax Australia Rules

  • Audax Australia Ride Rules
  • Brevet Permanent Rules
  • More Audax Australia information & resources

Summary of support that will be provided

  • Printable Maps & Cue sheets from this website.
  • GPX Track files (for most GPS) from this website.
  • Luggage support between overnight controls, max 15kg bag.
  • Overnight accommodation (all) 8 nights.
  • 4 Evening meals and (all) 8 breakfasts will be provided at (all) 4 overnight ride controls.
  • Basic snacks and water at intermediary controls.
  • Volunteer support at all overnight controls and at all intermediary controls.
  • Emergency transport in the event of mechanical breakdown or minor injury.
  • After ride breakfast, Tavern 42 Degrees South (T42).
